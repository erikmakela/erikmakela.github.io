Happy FOSS Friday, everyone! Let's shine a light on Baïkal, a lightweight CalDAV and CardDAV server enabling seamless synchronization of calendars and contacts.

*   **Simple Setup:** Baïkal boasts an intuitive web interface for effortless installation and configuration, even for non-technical users.
*   **Database Flexibility:** Choose between SQLite for smaller deployments or MySQL/MariaDB for enhanced scalability and performance.
*   **Open Source Advantage:** Benefit from a transparent, community-driven project, ensuring security, extensibility, and cost-effectiveness.
