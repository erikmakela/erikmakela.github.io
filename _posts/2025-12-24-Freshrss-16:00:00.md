For this week's FOSS Friday, let's explore FreshRSS, a self-hosted RSS aggregator perfect for taking control of your news feeds!

Here's what makes it stand out:

*   Its minimalist design and responsiveness provide a smooth reading experience across devices.
*   It's highly customizable, letting you tailor the interface and features to your needs.
*   It offers powerful filtering and organization tools to manage a large number of subscriptions effectively.

Here is where you can find their repository:

https://github.com/FreshRSS/FreshRSS
