Here is a great piece of software to consider this FOSS Friday!

Firefly III is a free and open source personal finance manager designed to help you track your income, expenses, and budget effectively.

*   Offers robust double-entry bookkeeping, giving a clear and accurate picture of your financial situation.
*   Provides a powerful rule engine to automate transaction categorization and tagging, saving you time and effort.
*   Supports importing data from various banks and financial institutions, simplifying the transition from other tools.

https://www.firefly-iii.org/
