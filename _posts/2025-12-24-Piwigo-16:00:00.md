Happy FOSS Friday, everyone! Today's featured open-source gem is Piwigo, a web photo gallery software built for organizations and individuals.

Here are a few standout qualities:

*   Piwigo offers fine-grained control over user permissions, ensuring secure and tailored access to your valuable image library.
*   Its robust plugin architecture extends functionality, letting you customize Piwigo to meet specific workflow and organizational needs.
*   Piwigo supports extensive metadata management, making it easy to organize and search through large photo collections with tags, descriptions, and more.

https://piwigo.org/
