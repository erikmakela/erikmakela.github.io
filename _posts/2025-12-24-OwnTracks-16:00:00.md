Happy FOSS Friday! Let's take a look at OwnTracks, a neat little app to keep track of where you are.

OwnTracks offers a privacy-focused solution for location tracking.

*   It allows you to privately host your location data, giving you complete control.
*   It supports MQTT for reliable communication, making it easy to integrate with home automation systems.
*   It is fully open-source and transparent.

https://owntracks.org/
