Happy FOSS Friday! Let's explore GoAccess, a fast and terminal-based web log analyzer.

Here's what makes GoAccess stand out:

*   Provides real-time web traffic statistics right in your terminal.
*   Offers various output formats, including HTML, JSON, and CSV, for easy integration.
*   Supports multiple log formats, including Apache, Nginx, and Amazon S3.

Website: https://goaccess.io
