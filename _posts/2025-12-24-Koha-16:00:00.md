It's FOSS Friday! Today, let's give a shout-out to Koha, a robust open-source Integrated Library System (ILS) empowering libraries worldwide.

Here are a few key aspects that make Koha a remarkable solution:

*   Complete Customization: Tailor Koha to perfectly reflect your library's unique workflow and branding thanks to its modular design and theming system.
*   Comprehensive Functionality: Benefit from a full suite of features including cataloging, circulation, acquisitions, serials management, and reporting, streamlining library operations.
*   Community Driven: Tap into a vibrant global community of developers, librarians, and vendors that guarantee continuous innovation, support, and collaborative development.

https://koha-community.org/
