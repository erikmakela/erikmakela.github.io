It's FOSS Friday! Let's spotlight Jitsi, a powerful open-source platform for video conferencing and collaboration.

Here's what sets Jitsi apart:

*   Effortlessly host or join meetings directly from your web browser, requiring no account creation or complicated setups.
*   Enjoy end-to-end encryption to ensure private, secure communication during all your discussions.
*   Customize and integrate Jitsi seamlessly into your existing applications and workflows thanks to its flexible open-source nature.

Check it out: https://jitsi.org/
