Here's your Friday recommendation for a brilliant open-source recipe manager.

Mealie is a streamlined and user-friendly recipe management system perfect for home cooks and culinary enthusiasts.

*   Effortlessly import recipes from websites with automatic metadata extraction.
*   Plan your meals efficiently with the built-in meal planning calendar and shopping list generation.
*   Customize and adapt recipes with ease, thanks to its intuitive editing tools.

https://hay-kot.github.io/mealie/
