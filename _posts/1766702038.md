Happy FOSS Friday! Let's spotlight Jitsi Meet, a fully encrypted, open-source video conferencing solution you can use directly in your browser.

*   Enjoy unparalleled privacy, thanks to its end-to-end encryption for one-on-one calls and strong encryption for multi-party calls.

*   Host or join meetings instantly, without needing to create an account, making collaboration quick and easy.

*   Customize your deployment by self-hosting or integrating it directly into your existing applications with its versatile API.
