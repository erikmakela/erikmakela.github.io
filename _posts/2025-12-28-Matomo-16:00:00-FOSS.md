Matomo is an open‑source web analytics platform that lets you track visitor behavior while keeping all data on your own servers. It offers the same depth of insight as commercial tools but with full transparency and privacy compliance.

- GDPR‑ready data ownership: all analytics stay on your own infrastructure.  
- Real‑time reporting: see who’s on your site now and what they’re doing.  
- Customizable dashboards & event tracking: build metrics that match your exact needs.  

[https://matomo.org/](https://matomo.org/)
