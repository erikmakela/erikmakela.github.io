BookStack is a free, open‑source knowledge‑management platform that lets teams create, organize, and share documentation in a clean, book‑style interface. It combines the familiarity of a library layout with powerful collaboration tools, making it easy to maintain structured content.

- WYSIWYG editor with Markdown support and inline image handling  
- Hierarchical navigation using books, chapters, and pages  
- Fine‑grained role‑based permissions and version history  

[https://bookstackapp.com](https://bookstackapp.com)
