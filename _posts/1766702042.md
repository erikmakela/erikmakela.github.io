Collaborate in real-time on documents with Etherpad, a lightweight and open-source online editor.

*   Simultaneous editing allows multiple users to work on the same document concurrently, seeing each other's changes instantly.
*   Color-coded authorship highlights each participant's contributions, making it easy to track individual input within the text.
*   Its simplicity and ease of deployment make it ideal for brainstorming, note-taking, and collaborative writing projects.

http://etherpad.org
