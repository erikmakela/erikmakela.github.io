Happy FOSS Friday, tech enthusiasts! I'm spotlighting Navidrome, a modern, lightweight, and container-friendly personal music streamer.

Here's why Navidrome stands out:

*   It flawlessly streams your entire music library, regardless of size, to any device.
*   It boasts seamless integration with a plethora of Subsonic-compatible players.
*   It presents a clean, intuitive web interface for easy management and discovery.
