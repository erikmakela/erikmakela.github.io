**Firefly III**  
Firefly III is a self‑hosted, open‑source personal finance manager that lets you track income, expenses, and budgets in a tidy, intuitive interface. It delivers detailed reports and a flexible rule engine that can be customized to fit your exact financial habits.

- **Robust budgeting tools**: Create, track, and forecast budgets with adjustable categories and sub‑categories.
- **Automated transaction import**: Pull data from a wide range of banks and file formats, then apply rules to keep records up to date.
- **Secure multi‑user support**: Share a household’s finances or collaborate with a small team while controlling access at the individual level.
