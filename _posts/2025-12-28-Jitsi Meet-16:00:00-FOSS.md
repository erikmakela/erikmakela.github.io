Jitsi Meet is a free, open‑source video conferencing platform that runs directly in your browser. It offers secure, end‑to-end encrypted meetings with no sign‑up needed, making it ideal for teams that need privacy and flexibility.

**Key features to highlight on FOSS Friday**

- End‑to-end encryption with optional passcodes for maximum security  
- Native screen‑sharing, recording, and chat—no plugins required  
- Fully customizable and embeddable in any web app, so you can integrate it into your own workflow  

[https://jitsi.org](https://jitsi.org)
