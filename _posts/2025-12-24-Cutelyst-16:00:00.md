For FOSS Friday, let's spotlight Cutelyst!

Cutelyst is a high-performance web framework built on Qt, designed for creating scalable and maintainable web applications.

Here's what makes it stand out:

*   Offers a non-blocking I/O model allowing efficient handling of concurrent requests.
*   Integrates seamlessly with Qt, providing access to a wide range of libraries and tools.
*   Emphasizes code reusability and maintainability through its plugin-based architecture.

https://cutelyst.org/
